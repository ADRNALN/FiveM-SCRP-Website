About Us

SCRP is a semi-realistic FiveM roleplay server. Our server consists of multiple custom vehicles, scripts, and maps.
Some of our first responders are real life first responders which helps us bring our police, fire, and ems
roleplay to a whole another level.

Departments

SCRP has one main police department which is the San Andreas State Police. We also have a Dispatcher role and a
Fire/EMS department. Recently we added S.W.A.T, Undercover, Coast Guard, and AirOne as sub departments
members can join as well, after they have been accepted into one of the other departments.

Features

We have a multitude of features from a full functioning CAD/MDT System, EUP, vMenu, custom vehicles, and
a custom map! We also have many realistic weapon replacements and a realistic driving script, that'll
challenge even the most seasoned criminal.
